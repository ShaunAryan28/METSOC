About the Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes from Marvel Comics, known for protecting the world from various threats. They were created by writer and editor Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby, making their debut in "The Avengers" #1 in 1963.

The team includes iconic characters such as Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and many others. Each Avenger has unique superpowers and abilities that they use to defend the Earth from supervillains, aliens, and other dangers.

The Avengers have appeared in numerous comic book series, animated TV shows, and blockbuster movies. Their popularity has made them a cultural phenomenon, and they have a dedicated fan base around the world.
